## üöÄ Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Access the System

**Desktop/Laptop:**
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. Navigate to: **https://your-institution-domain.edu/sms**
3. You should see the Student Management System login page

**Mobile Device:**
1. Open your device's browser (same browsers as above)
2. Navigate to: **https://your-institution-domain.edu/sms**
3. The system is mobile-friendly and will adapt to your screen

### Step 2: Log In

1. Enter your **username** or **email address** in the first field
2. Enter your **password** in the second field
3. Click **"Sign In"** button
4. You will be redirected to your personal dashboard

**First Time Login?**
- Contact your institution's IT help desk for your initial username and temporary password
- On first login, you will be prompted to set a new password
- **Save your password securely** - you'll need it to log in each time

### Step 3: Explore Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows different information depending on your role:

**For Students:**
- Current grades and GPA
- Enrolled courses
- Attendance record
- Upcoming assignments
- Messages from instructors

**For Teachers:**
- Student roster
- Grade entry interface
- Attendance tracking
- Course materials
- Reporting tools

**For Administrators:**
- System health and status
- User management options
- Report generation
- System configuration

## üÜò Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address or username
3. Check your email for a password reset link (within 10 minutes)
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

**If you don't receive the reset email:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait 10 minutes and try again
- Contact your IT helpdesk

## üíæ How to Use Search

**Finding Students/Courses/Grades:**

1. Look for the search box (usually top of page or in each section)
2. Type the name, ID, or code you're looking for
3. Results appear automatically as you type
4. Click the result to view details

**Search Tips:**
- Search is not case-sensitive (JOHN = john)
- Partial names work (typing "Joh" finds "John Smith")
- Use quotes for exact matches: "John Smith"
- Use filters alongside search for precise results

## üñ®Ô∏è Exporting and Printing

**How to Export Data:**

1. Find the data you want to export (grades, attendance, etc.)
2. Look for **"Export"** button (usually top-right)
3. Select format:
   - **PDF** - for viewing and printing
   - **Excel** - for analysis and calculations
   - **CSV** - for other systems/tools
4. Click **"Download"** or **"Export"**
5. File will download to your computer

**How to Print:**
1. View the page or export to PDF
2. Press **Ctrl+P** (Windows) or **Cmd+P** (Mac)
3. Select your printer and settings
4. Click **"Print"**
